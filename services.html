Garage Floor Epoxy Coating

The most popular service we offer in Yuma is a full broadcast flake system for residential garage floors. A professionally applied flake epoxy system seals all of that out.

We offer solid-color epoxy, partial flake, and full-broadcast systems in dozens of color blends. Metallic Epoxy Flooring

Metallic epoxy uses mica and aluminum pigment blended into a clear epoxy base, then manipulated with airflow and rollers to create swirling, three-dimensional patterns. No two metallic floors look the same, which is why they’re popular for Yuma showrooms, home studios, high-end man caves, and garages where the floor is part of the statement.

The installation process is more demanding than standard flake systems—metallic pigment requires precise product ratios and faster work to control the pattern before the epoxy begins to set. In Yuma, that time window is shorter due to heat. We apply metallic systems only in optimal temperature conditions and always finish with a UV-resistant polyaspartic topcoat to keep the metallic sheen looking sharp long-term. Call us today for metallic epoxy flooring in Yuma, AZ.

Flake & Chip Broadcast Systems

Full-broadcast vinyl chip systems are one of the most practical choices for Yuma garages and workspaces. The vinyl flake covers minor surface blemishes and pitting that would show through a solid-color system, adds natural slip resistance for a safer work surface, and creates a professional, speckled appearance that hides dirt and tire marks between cleanings.

We stock a wide range of flake blends from neutral grays and tans to bold multi-color mixes. The broadcast is sealed with a matte or gloss polyaspartic topcoat depending on your preference. Most Yuma flake jobs complete in a single day, with light foot traffic possible the next morning. Call us today for flake epoxy flooring in Yuma, AZ.

Concrete Grinding & Surface Prep

Surface preparation is what separates a floor that lasts 12 years from one that peels in 18 months. We perform mechanical diamond grinding on every project—residential and commercial—to open the concrete’s pore structure and create a CSP (concrete surface profile) that allows the coating to penetrate and bond properly.

We also offer concrete grinding as a standalone service for homeowners who want to remove an existing failed coating before applying a new one themselves, or who need surface prep before staining or polishing. Call (928) 817-8550 for concrete grinding in Yuma, AZ.
